Voice Agent Animations

Lightweight, GPU-friendly status animations for a voice assistant. Previews auto-cycle idle → listening → working → speaking every 4 seconds, or use the floating switch below to pin every preview to one state at once. Click a card to open it full-screen and drive the states yourself (buttons, keys 1–4, or click to cycle).
